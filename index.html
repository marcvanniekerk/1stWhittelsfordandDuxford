Who we are

We are the 1st Whittlesford and Duxford Scouts, part of the UK’s biggest mixed youth organisation.
We change lives by offering 6- to 14-year-olds fun and challenging activities, unique experiences, everyday adventure and skills for life.
Scouts helps children and young adults reach their full potential. We help young people to get jobs, save lives and even change the world.
